        <h3>Introduction</h3>
        <p>The current merchandise purchasing process at the University of Vocational Technology relies on Google Forms, with payments made by submitting bank slips...</p>
        
        <h3>Objective</h3>
        <ul>
            <li>To view and purchase university merchandise for each faculty, department, and club.</li>
            <li>To create an account using their university email and password.</li>
            <li>To browse available merchandise with complete details, including designs, prices, and size charts.</li>
            <li>To place orders using an online payment system.</li>
        </ul>
